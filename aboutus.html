Fighting For Our Rights

Des Moines, Iowa Area
We are a legal assistance and life coaching Company that helps all members of the LGBTQ, as well as Support and Advocates to the Transgender community.
Brandie DeVore Has been independently studying LGBTQ law for several years and have a very good knowledge of the procedures. She is also a researcher of Educational meterial, Legal Proceedings and cases. We will file discrimination and Civil Rights complaints when the Rights of the LGBTQ community members have been violated.
We believe in equal rights for everyone.
We offer our services and support for those that need it at their request.
